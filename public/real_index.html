Visualize where you have been in a single map

You can upload GPX file directly through your browser. Apple account is required. Register an Apple account and start Tracing today, even without iOS devices!

Sign in with Apple account

Categorize your traces. Show you what you want.

Seven most typical types of traces are defined, which are, Walking, Cycling, Running, Bus/Car, Train, Boating and Flight. Different types are marked with different colors. You can select which types to show on your map.

Mark your places. Visited or yet to be.

Mark locations as Visited or Want to visit. You know where to go during your next trip! Traces will also automatically count the countries you visited.

Secured by Apple's iCloud. Your data is safe.

Your data is processed at client side and saved in iCloud. We don't have any server handling your data. Apple made apps, such as Photos, Notes, Contacts, Messages, all use the same iCloud technology as Traces does. Without your authorization, even the developers cannot access your data.
